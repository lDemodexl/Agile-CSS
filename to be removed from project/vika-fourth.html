Our Services

Brooke Voss is a full service decorating house that is known for building candid and deep relationships with our clients, be it individuals or companies, assisting them in crafting their ideal living and working space.

Our team of qualified interior decorators and interior designers provide a very unique and personal service to each client, based on the clients brief and particular needs. All projects are meticulously curated and expertly executed from conception through to styling.

WE FOCUS ON

  • Space Planning
  • Decorator Services
  • Interior Design
  • Custom Furniture Design
  • Turnkey Interior Design projects

Each Client Has A Unique Vision And Our Role Is To Transform That Idea Into A Reality While Elevating It To Its Greatest Potential.

Décor

  • Survey and measurements for quotation purposes only
  • No drawings, furniture layouts or presentations

Look & Feel Presentation

  • Client to supply architect/floor plans
  • Site visit and consultation based on client’s needs
  • A4 document with proposed furniture layout
  • Images of furnishings to aid visualisation of project
  • R3000

Design

  • Interior design presentation
  • Furniture placement plan based on original architect plans
  • Colour scheme and fabrics selection
  • Presentation boards including selected 3D renders
  • Fee is 10% of the final project value
  • Up to 100% refundable against furniture purchases over R1 000 000

3D Renders On Full Interior Design

  • R5000 per view (for additional renders)
  • Renders or 3D drawings presentations to convey a sense of space, style and hues of a project

Custom Furniture Design

  • R500 per hour
  • In addition to showcasing International furniture and décor products, Casarredo also designs custom furniture pieces to match a client’s specific requirements. Existing products can be custom designed to fit any space.

Turnkey Interior Design

  • Full interior design presentation, including selecting finishes (floor and wall), fittings and built in units
  • Furniture, fittings and unitary placement plan based on original architect plans
  • Colour scheme based on flooring and decor selection
  • Presentation boards with renders or 3D drawings to convey a sense of space, style and hues of a project
  • Fee is 10% of the final project value
  • Up to 100% refundable against furniture and fixture purchases over R2 000 000

*First invoice will be based on an estimate and adjusted accordingly.
